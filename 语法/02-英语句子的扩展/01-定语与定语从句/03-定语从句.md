# 定语从句

## 📖 怎么理解定语从句

定语从句就是把一个完整的句子拿过来修饰名词。前面学的定语词和短语只能表达简单的修饰，而定语从句可以表达**复杂、完整的信息**。

> 画面感：定语词和短语像给名词"穿衣服"，而定语从句像给名词"讲一个故事"。
>
> - 短语：the girl **in red** → 穿红衣服的女孩（一个画面）
> - 从句：the girl **who saved my life** → 救过我命的那个女孩（一段故事）

---

## 一、定语从句基本结构

### 三个核心概念

```
This is the book   that    I bought yesterday.
      ↓            ↓           ↓
    先行词       关系词        从句
（被修饰的名词）（连接词）  （修饰内容）
```

| 术语 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **先行词** | 被定语从句修饰的名词/代词 | the **book** |
| **关系词** | 连接先行词和从句的词 | **that** |
| **从句** | 修饰先行词的句子 | I bought yesterday |

### 关系词在从句中充当一个成分

这是理解定语从句最关键的一点——**关系词在从句里是有"职位"的**：

```
This is the book that I bought yesterday.
                 └── 关系词 that 在从句中作 bought 的宾语
                 → I bought the book yesterday.
```

```
The man who lives next door is a doctor.
        └── 关系词 who 在从句中作主语
        → The man lives next door.
```

## 二、关系代词

关系代词在从句中充当**主语、宾语、定语**，代替先行词。

### 总览表

| 关系代词 | 先行词 | 在从句中充当 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **who** | 人 | 主语/宾语 | the man **who** helped me |
| **whom** | 人 | 宾语 | the man **whom** I met |
| **whose** | 人/物 | 定语（……的） | the boy **whose** father is a doctor |
| **which** | 物/动物 | 主语/宾语 | the book **which** I read |
| **that** | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 | the book **that** I read |

---

### 2.1 who —— 指人，作主语或宾语

| 从句中职位 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **作主语** | The man **who** lives next door is kind. |
| **作宾语** | The man **who** I met yesterday is kind.（口语，= whom） |

> 拆解：The man **who** lives next door...
>       先行词   └─ who 是 lives 的主语，代替 the man

---

### 2.2 whom —— 指人，只作宾语

| 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| The man **whom** I met is kind. | whom 作 met 的宾语 ← I met **the man** |
| The girl **with whom** I went is my friend. | 介词后必须用 whom |

> 口语中 whom 常被 who 替代，但**介词后面只能用 whom**：
> - ✅ the man **with whom** I went
> - ❌ the man **with who** I went

---

### 2.3 whose —— 指人或物，作定语（……的）

**这是中国学生最容易忽略的关系词**，因为它不表示"谁"，而是"谁的/什么的"。

| 先行词 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 指人 | The boy **whose** father is a doctor studies hard. |
| 指物 | I like the house **whose** windows are large. |

> 拆解：The boy **whose** father is a doctor...
>       = The boy's father is a doctor...（whose = the boy's）
>
> whose 就等于**先行词加 's**。

**指物时，whose 可以替换为 of which：**

> the house **whose** windows are large
> = the house **the windows of which** are large
> = the house **of which the windows** are large

---

### 2.4 which —— 指物，作主语或宾语

| 从句中职位 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **作主语** | The book **which** is on the desk is mine. |
| **作宾语** | The book **which** I bought is interesting. |

---

### 2.5 that —— 万能词，指人或物，作主语或宾语

| 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| The man **that** helped me is kind. | that = who |
| The book **that** I bought is good. | that = which |

> that 在日常英语中经常可以替换 who/which，但有些情况只能用它（见后面专题）。

## 三、关系副词

关系副词在从句中充当**状语**，表示时间、地点、原因。

| 关系副词 | 先行词 | 在从句中充当 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **when** | 时间名词 | 时间状语 | I remember the day **when** we met. |
| **where** | 地点名词 | 地点状语 | This is the house **where** I grew up. |
| **why** | reason | 原因状语 | Tell me the reason **why** you left. |

### 怎么区分用关系代词还是关系副词？

**看从句里缺什么成分：**

```
缺主语/宾语 → 关系代词（who/which/that）
缺状语     → 关系副词（when/where/why）
```

| 对比 | 从句拆解 | 判断 |
|------|------|------|
| This is the factory **that** I visited. | I visited **the factory**.（缺宾语）→ 用 that |
| This is the factory **where** I work. | I work **in the factory**.（缺地点状语）→ 用 where |

> 口诀：**从句完整用副词，从句缺东西用代词**

### 关系副词 = 介词 + which

| 关系副词 | 替换为介词 + which |
|------|------|
| when | **on/in/at which** |
| where | **in/at which** |
| why | **for which** |

> I remember the day **when** we met.
> = I remember the day **on which** we met.
>
> This is the house **where** I grew up.
> = This is the house **in which** I grew up.

## 四、定语从句中的介词前置 ★ 考研长难句重点

### 📖 怎么理解

介词在定语从句中有两个位置：可以留在从句末尾，也可以提前到关系词前面。**提前是书面语，考研阅读和翻译中极常见。**

> The girl **who** I went **with** is my friend.   （介词在末尾，口语）
> The girl **with whom** I went is my friend.     （介词提前，书面）
>
> 介词一提，句子立刻变正式。

### 为什么要有介词前置？

英语书面语追求**紧凑、不松散**。介词放在从句末尾显得"拖泥带水"，提到前面后整个从句更紧凑，也更像"高级英语"。

```
This is the problem   which   we talked about.      （松散）
This is the problem  about which  we talked.         （紧凑）
```

### 介词前置的规则

| 规则 | 正确 | 错误 |
|------|------|------|
| 介词后面只能用 **whom**（人） | with **whom** | ~~with who~~ |
| 介词后面只能用 **which**（物） | about **which** | ~~about that~~ |
| 介词后面不能用 **that** | for **which** | ~~for that~~ |
| 固定动词短语的介词一般不拆 | look after the baby | ~~the baby after whom I look~~ ❌ |

> **固定短语不能拆**：look after（照顾）、look for（寻找）、take care of（照顾）、put up with（忍受）等是整体，硬拆开反而不自然。
>
> ✅ The baby **who** I look **after** is cute.
> ❌ The baby **after whom** I look is cute.（语法没错但不自然）

### 介词怎么选？

**看从句动词和先行词之间的逻辑关系：**

| 例句 | 介词来源 | 逻辑 |
|------|------|------|
| The man **with whom** I went... | go **with** the man | 和……一起 |
| The problem **about which** we talked... | talk **about** the problem | 关于…… |
| The tool **with which** he fixed it... | fix it **with** the tool | 用…… |
| The company **for which** I work... | work **for** the company | 为了…… |
| The speed **at which** he runs... | run **at** a speed | 以…… |
| The reason **for which** he left... | leave **for** the reason | 因为…… |

> 方法：把从句还原成正常语序，看先行词前面应该放什么介词。

### 介词前置 vs 关系副词

本质上是一回事，只是表达方式不同：

| 关系副词 | 介词 + which | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| when | **on/in/at which** | 在……时候 |
| where | **in/at/on which** | 在……地方 |
| why | **for which** | 因为……原因 |

> I remember the day **when** we met.
> = I remember the day **on which** we met.（介词前置版）
>
> 关系副词 = 介词 + which，是介词前置的一种"合并写法"。

### 考研长难句示例

```
This is the environment in which the child grows up.
                             └── 介词 + which ──┘
= 这就是孩子在其中长大的环境。
→ 更顺的翻译：这就是孩子长大的环境。
```

```
The manner in which he answered the question surprised me.
                               └──── 介词 + which ────┘
= 他回答问题的那种方式让我惊讶。
```

> 翻译技巧：`介词 + which + 从句` → 把从句和先行词合起来，介词融进去翻译，不要硬翻"在其中/通过那种"。

## 五、限制性 vs 非限制性定语从句 ★ 考研重点

### 📖 怎么理解

| | 限制性 | 非限制性 |
|------|------|------|
| **作用** | **圈定范围**——不说是哪个你就不知道 | **补充信息**——不说你也知道是谁 |
| **逗号** | 没有逗号 | **有逗号**隔开 |
| **去掉后** | 意思不明确 | 意思仍然完整 |
| **翻译** | ……的…… | 另起一句 |

### 对比两组

```
限制性：My brother who lives in Beijing is a doctor.
        └────── 圈定范围 ──────┘
        我住在北京的那个哥哥是医生。（我有好几个哥哥，特指北京那个）

非限制：My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor.
        └────── 补充信息 ──────┘
        我哥哥是医生，他住在北京。（我只有一个哥哥，顺便说他住北京）
```

### 非限制性不能用 that

| ✅ | ❌ |
|------|------|
| The book, **which** I bought yesterday, is good. | The book, **that** I bought yesterday, is good. |
| My mother, **who** is a teacher, supports me. | My mother, **that** is a teacher, supports me. |

### which 可以指代整个主句（非限制性特有）

> He passed the exam, **which** surprised everyone.
> 他通过了考试，**这件事**让所有人惊讶。
>
> → which 不指代 exam，而指代"他通过了考试"这整件事。

## 六、介词 + 关系代词（深度拓展）

### 📖 怎么理解

介词本来在从句末尾，提到关系词前面就变成了"介词 + which/whom"结构。书面语和考研阅读中极常见。

```
This is the house   which   I live in.        （口语）
This is the house  in which  I live.          （书面）
                    ↑ 介词提前
```

### 介词怎么选？

**看从句动词搭配，或者看与先行词的关系。**

| 例句 | 介词来源 |
|------|------|
| The man **with whom** I went... | go **with** the man |
| The problem **about which** we talked... | talk **about** the problem |
| The tool **with which** he fixed it... | fix it **with** the tool |
| The company **for which** I work... | work **for** the company |
| The speed **at which** he runs... | run **at** a speed |

> 技巧：把从句还原成正常语序，看动词和先行词之间需要什么介词。
>
> → I work **for** the company → the company **for which** I work
> → I live **in** the house → the house **in which** I live

## 七、只能用 that 的情况 ★ 完形填空高频

| 情况 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **1. 先行词被最高级修饰** | This is the best film **that** I have ever seen. |
| **2. 先行词被序数词修饰** | He is the first person **that** arrived. |
| **3. 先行词是不定代词** (all, everything, nothing, anything, little, much 等) | All **that** I want is your love. |
| **4. 先行词被 the only/very/same 修饰** | This is the very book **that** I want. |
| **5. 先行词既有人又有物** | They talked of the people and things **that** they remembered. |
| **6. 主句以 who/which 开头的疑问句** | Who is the man **that** is standing there? |

> 口诀：**最高、序数、不定代，唯一正是人加物，疑问避免重复用**

## 八、关系词的省略

关系词在从句中**作宾语**时，可以省略。

| 可省略 | 不可省略 |
|------|------|
| The book **(that)** I bought is good. ← that 作宾语 | The book **that** is on the desk is good. ← that 作主语 |

> 判断标准：关系词后面直接跟**动词** → 作主语，不可省；关系词后面跟**名词/代词** → 作宾语，可省。
>
> - ...the book **that is** on the desk → that + 动词 is → 主语，不可省
> - ...the book **that I** bought → that + 名词 I → 宾语，可省

---

## 九、as 引导的定语从句

as 也可以引导定语从句，常与 such、the same 搭配，或引导非限制性从句。

| 用法 | 例句 | 翻译 |
|------|------|------|
| **such...as** | I like such books **as** are interesting. | 我喜欢那些有趣的书。 |
| **the same...as** | This is the same book **as** I lost. | 这和我丢的那本书一样。 |
| **as 指代整句**（非限制性） | **As** we all know, the earth is round. / The earth is round, **as** we all know. | 众所周知，地球是圆的。 |

> as vs which（指代整句时）：
> - as 位置灵活（句首/句中/句末），which 只能放句末
> - as 有"正如"的含义，which 只是"这件事"

## 十、定语从句解题三步法

### 第一步：找先行词

确定定语从句修饰的是谁。

### 第二步：看从句缺什么成分

```
缺主语/宾语 → 关系代词（who/which/that/whom）
缺定语      → whose
缺状语      → 关系副词（when/where/why）+ 判断介词可替换
```

### 第三步：看是否有限制条件

```
有逗号       → 非限制性 → 不能用 that
最高级/序数词 → 限制性   → 优先用 that
介词提前     → 只能用 which/whom（不能用 that/who）
```

### 长难句拆解示例

```
The man who I thought was my friend turned out to be a stranger.
│       └───────── 定语从句 ─────────┘                              │
└── 先行词                                                    └── 谓语

拆从句：who I thought was my friend
       └─ who 是 was 的主语（I thought 是插入语，先忽略）
还原：The man was my friend.
```

## 十一、练习

### 一、选择正确的关系词

1. This is the house ___where_____ I grew up. (which / where / who)
2. The girl ___whose_____ father is a pilot wants to fly. (who / whose / which)
3. He passed the exam, ____which____ made his parents proud. (that / which / who)
4. This is the best movie ___that_____ I have ever seen. (which / that / who)
5. The man with ___whom_____ I went fishing is my uncle. (who / whom / which)
6. I still remember the day ___when_____ we first met. (when / which / where)
7. They talked of the people and things ___that_____ they missed. (which / who / that)
8. Tell me the reason ____why____ you were late. (which / why / when)

### 二、判断对错并改正

1. This is the house which I was born.  -》 where
2. She is the girl which I told you about. whom
3. All which you need is love.  that
4. My father, that is a teacher, loves reading. who
5. This is the book whom I borrowed from the library. which

### 三、合并句子（用定语从句）

1. I met a man. The man can speak five languages. I met a man who can speak five languages.
2. This is the school. I studied there for three years. -》This is the school where I studied for three years.
3. The movie was boring. I watched it last night. The movie which I watched  last night was boring
4. She lost a necklace. The necklace was made of gold. -》 She lost a necklace which was made of gold
5. Do you remember the day? We went to the beach together that day. -》 Do you remember the day when We went to the beach together

## 参考答案

### 一、选择关系词
1. **where**（从句完整 I grew up，缺地点状语 → 关系副词）
2. **whose**（父亲的……→ 定语 → whose = the girl's）
3. **which**（逗号 → 非限制性，不能用 that；指代整件事）
4. **that**（最高级 best → 只能用 that）
5. **whom**（介词 with 后面 → 只能用 whom）
6. **when**（从句完整 we met，缺时间状语 → 关系副词）
7. **that**（先行词既有 people 又有 things → 只能用 that）
8. **why**（先行词是 reason，缺原因状语 → why = for which）

### 二、改错
1. ❌ which → ✅ **where** / **in which**（从句 I was born 完整，缺地点状语）
2. ❌ which → ✅ **who** / **that**（先行词 girl 指人）
3. ❌ which → ✅ **that**（先行词是不定代词 all，只能用 that）
4. ❌ that → ✅ **who**（非限制性定语从句不能用 that）
5. ❌ whom → ✅ **which** / **that**（先行词 book 指物）

### 三、合并句子
1. I met a man **who can speak** five languages.
2. This is the school **where I studied** for three years. / **in which I studied**...
3. The movie **which I watched** last night was boring.
4. She lost a necklace **which/that was made of** gold.
5. Do you remember the day **when we went to** the beach together?
