# 宾语从句

## 📖 怎么理解宾语从句

宾语从句就是在**及物动词、介词或某些形容词后面**用一个完整的句子来充当宾语。

> 画面感：如果主语从句是把"一件事"当主语，宾语从句就是把"一件事"当宾语。
>
> - 简单宾语：I know **the answer**. 我知道答案。
> - 宾语从句：I know **that he is right**. 我知道他是对的。

---

## 一、宾语从句的位置

宾语从句可以放在三个位置：

| 位置 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **动词后面**（最常见） | I think **that he is right**. |
| **介词后面** | He is interested in **what I said**. |
| **某些形容词后面** | I am afraid **that I can't come**. |

---

## 二、that 引导的宾语从句

### 基本结构

```
I think    that    he is right.
↓          ↓       └── 从句（陈述句语序）──┘
主语+谓语  连接词
```

### that 可以省略

宾语从句中 that 经常省略，这是和主语从句最大的区别之一。

| 完整 | 省略 |
|------|------|
| I think **that** he is right. | I think he is right. ✅ |
| She said **that** she would come. | She said she would come. ✅ |

### 什么时候不能省 that？

| 情况 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **1. 两个并列的宾语从句** | I know **that** he is kind and **that** he is honest. （第二个 that 必须保留） |
| **2. it 作形式宾语** | I think **it** important **that** you come. |
| **3. 从句和动词中间插入其他成分** | I believe, however, **that** he is right. |

### 宾语从句中 that 的时态规则

**主句过去时 → 宾语从句时态往后退（时态一致）：**

| 直接引语 | 转述（主句过去时） |
|------|------|
| He said, "I **am** happy." | He said that he **was** happy. |
| He said, "I **will** come." | He said that he **would** come. |
| He said, "I **have finished**." | He said that he **had finished**. |

> 例外：从句是**客观真理**时，时态不变：
> The teacher said that the earth **moves** around the sun.

---

## 三、whether / if 引导的宾语从句

whether 和 if 都表示 **"是否"**，在宾语从句中**两者都可用**（注意要和主语从句区分）。

| 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| I don't know **whether / if** he will come. | 我不知道他是否会来。 |
| She asked **whether / if** I was free. | 她问我是否有空。 |

### 只能用 whether，不能用 if 的情况

| 情况 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **1. 介词后面** | It depends on **whether** he comes. (❌ if) |
| **2. 后面跟 or not 紧接** | I don't know **whether or not** he will come. (❌ if or not) |
| **3. 不定式前面** | I don't know **whether to** go. (❌ if to) |

> 口诀：**宾语从句 whether/if 都可，介词后只能用 whether**

---

## 四、连接代词引导的宾语从句

和主语从句一样，连接代词在从句中**充当成分**：

| 引导词 | 例句 | 在从句中的职位 |
|------|------|------|
| **what** | I know **what he wants**. | wants 的宾语 |
| **who** | I wonder **who did this**. | did 的主语 |
| **whom** | Tell me **whom you trust**. | trust 的宾语 |
| **which** | I can't decide **which is better**. | is 的主语 |
| **whoever** | I'll accept **whoever you choose**. | choose 的宾语 |

### what 宾语从句（最常考）

```
I don't understand what you said.
│             └──── 宾语从句 ────┘
└── 主句

拆从句：you said what → what 是 said 的宾语
```

---

## 五、连接副词引导的宾语从句

| 引导词 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **when** | Please tell me **when we will start**. |
| **where** | I wonder **where he lives**. |
| **why** | He didn't explain **why he was late**. |
| **how** | She showed me **how it works**. |

### 注意：宾语从句的语序 ★

**宾语从句永远用陈述句语序（主语 + 谓语），不可以用疑问句语序：**

| ✅ 正确 | ❌ 错误 |
|------|------|
| I don't know **where he lives**. | I don't know **where does he live**. |
| Tell me **what he said**. | Tell me **what did he say**. |
| He asked **when we would arrive**. | He asked **when would we arrive**. |

> 这是考研改错和完形填空的高频考点！中国学生习惯把疑问词后面倒装，但在宾语从句中**不能倒装**。

---

## 六、介词宾语从句

介词后面也可以跟宾语从句：

| 例句 | 介词 |
|------|------|
| He is proud of **what he has achieved**. | of |
| She is thinking about **whether she should go**. | about |
| The problem lies in **how we can solve it**. | in |
| We talked about **who should be the leader**. | about |

> 注意：介词后面**不能用 that 宾语从句**：
> ❌ He is proud of **that** he won.
> ✅ He is proud of **the fact that** he won.（加一个名词作桥梁）

---

## 七、形容词 + 宾语从句

| 常见形容词 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| afraid | I'm afraid **that I can't come**. |
| sure / certain | I'm sure **that he will succeed**. |
| glad / happy | I'm glad **that you made it**. |
| surprised | He was surprised **that she knew**. |
| aware | I'm aware **that it's difficult**. |

---

## 八、it 作形式宾语

当宾语从句后面有**宾补**时，英语用 it 占位，真宾语从句后移：

```
I think it important that you come on time.
      └形式宾语┘  └── 真实宾语从句 ──┘

= I think that you come on time is important.
          （语法没错但英语从不这样表达）
```

| 常见句式 | 翻译 |
|------|------|
| I think **it** important **that**... | 我认为……是重要的 |
| I find **it** strange **that**... | 我觉得……很奇怪 |
| We consider **it** necessary **that**... | 我们认为……是必要的 |
| I take **it** for granted **that**... | 我想当然地认为…… |

## 九、练习

### 一、翻译

1. 我不知道他住在哪里。
2. 她问我是否会来。
3. 你能告诉我你看到了什么吗？
4. 我认为准时来很重要。（用 it 形式宾语）
5. 关键在于我们如何解决这个问题。

### 二、改错

1. I don't know where does he live. -> I don't know where he live.
2. Tell me what did he say. -> tell me what he say.
3. It depends on if he will come. -> It depends on wether he will come.
4. I'm sure about that he is honest. -> I'm sure that he is honest.
5. I wonder that he will come or not. -> I wonder wether he will come

---

## 参考答案

### 一、翻译
1. I don't know **where he lives**.
2. She asked me **whether/if I would come**.
3. Can you tell me **what you saw**?
4. I think **it** important **that** you come on time.
5. The key lies in **how we can solve the problem**.

### 二、改错
1. ❌ where does he live → ✅ **where he lives**（陈述句语序）
2. ❌ what did he say → ✅ **what he said**（陈述句语序）
3. ❌ if → ✅ **whether**（介词后面只能用 whether）
4. ❌ about that → ✅ **删掉 about**（介词后不能直接接 that 从句）
5. ❌ that → ✅ **whether**（or not → 只能用 whether）
